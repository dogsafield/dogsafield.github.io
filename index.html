DogsAfield is a social app that helps dog owners discover, host, and attend local meetups — pack walks, picnics, and field games. Browse a live map of nearby events, RSVP with one tap, and after the walk a quick roll call confirms who you met, unlocking direct messaging with your new packmates. Your location is used only to show you nearby events — it's never stored or tracked in the background.
